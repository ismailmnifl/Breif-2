Drama- Performance Poetry

Posted in Year 5/6 on Thursday, 21st November 2019

In drama this week, linked to the recent frosty weather, we have been reading a poem called "Snowflake". The children performed the poem in groups, showing understanding through intonation, tone and volume. 


Snowflake

Six-sided symmetry, 

Dendrite, hexagonal plate, 

Like fingerprints, unique. 

Soft, seemingly weightless, 

Crystal clear coldness. 

Frozen, frosted, fragile. 

A moment of beauty,

Before it melts away, 

Gone, perhaps forever.
